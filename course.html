Courses Offered

courses we offer

Beginners In Magic

Core Courses like Transfiguration, Charms, Potions, History of Magic, Defence Against the Dark Arts, Astronomy and Herbology are taught . Students will be able to explore in all type of magic. Flying lessons (on broomsticks) are also compulsory.

Intermediate In Magic

In depth teaching of course will be done. Students were required to choose a minimum of two additional subjects from the following list: Arithmancy, Muggle Studies, Divination, Study of Ancient Runes and Care of Magical Creatures

Graduation In Magic

Specific subjects for N.E.W.T. level required for the job, taking into account their predicted O.W.L. grades based on their current performance, will be taught. This course will make the student more experienced and at the end of the course the student will be an expert in magic.

Post Graduation In Magic

Students advanced to N.E.W.T.-level classes, based on the individual's O.W.L. scores for a certain subject.Course work during this year was much more advanced than it had been previously.Students could also elect to take part in Advanced Apparition lessons

Ph.D in Magic

Very specialised subjects such as Alchemy were offered in the final two years.Advanced teaching of the subject selected will be done. Once the course is completed , the student will be able to also teach with accurate practices.
